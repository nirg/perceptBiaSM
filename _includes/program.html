Program

Session 1 (8:30-10:30)

  • 8:30-8:40: Introduction - Introduce the organizers and provide an outline of the workshop's activities.

  • 8:40-9:00: Meet workshop participants - Allow all participants to introduce themselves, their research interests and their interests in the topic of the workshop.

  • 9:00-9:40: Keynote 1 - Dr. Alice Marwick

  • 9:40-10:15: Break-out groups: Participants will organize into groups of 3-4 people and discuss the various perceptual biases raised by workshop submissions, determining which they think are the most important.

  • 10:15-10:30: Discussion of group results and collective decision on which biases to include in the paper.

Coffee break (10:30-11:00)

Session 2 (11:00-12:30)

  • 11:00-11:40: Keynote 2 - Dr. Christo Wilson

  • 11:40-12:15: Break-out groups: Participants will organize into different groups of 3-4 people and discuss Questions 2-4 with a focus on perceptual biases of particular importance.

  • 12:15-12:30: Discussion and conclusions - Including determining a path for finalization of the position paper and submission details.
