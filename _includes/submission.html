Call for Participation

We seek one-page reflections as submissions. These will assist in creating an outline for the position paper. All submissions should be one page or less and should contain two parts:

  1. A brief outline of a perceptual biases that impacts the way one or more social media systems are used and/or that are impacted by the use of social media.
  2. A brief sketch of a potential project or an existing work that addresses Question 2, 3 or 4 from the above themes with respect to this bias and one or more social media systems.

Submissions will be non-archival. The final position paper will be published in a venue chosen by workshop participants. Anyone who makes a submission and attends the workshop will be invited as contributing author to the position paper.
